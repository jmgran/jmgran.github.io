I am a professor of biostatistics at the [Oslo Centre of Biostatistics and Epidemiology](https://www.med.uio.no/imb/english/research/centres/ocbe/), Department of Biostatistics, [University of Oslo](https://www.uio.no), working on causal inference and time-to-event analysis.

I have PhD in biostatistics from the University of Oslo in 2011 and a MSc in Industrial Mathematics from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology in Trondheim from 2005. I have previously had positions at the Section for Biostatistics and Epidemiology at the Oslo University Hospital and at the Department of Infectious Disease Epidemiology at the Norwegian Institute of Public Health. 

I am currently an associate editor in [Biometrika](https://academic.oup.com/biomet).

See also my [personal web pages at the University of Oslo](https://www.med.uio.no/imb/english/people/aca/jonmic/index.html) and the web pages of our [causal inference group](https://www.med.uio.no/imb/english/research/groups/causal-inference-methods/index.html).
